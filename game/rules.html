RULES

Introduction
Welcome to Tez! Get ready for a thrilling, fast-paced racing experience where you navigate the challenges of EV life and race against the clock to keep your battery alive for two intense minutes. With sharp turns, unexpected surprises, and edge-of-your-seat excitement, we hope you enjoy the ride!
Rule 1: Collect Batteries
Collect batteries as you progress through the game to keep your car running. If you fail to collect enough batteries, your car's power will deplete, and the game will end early.
Rule 2: Dodge Cars
Avoid hitting cars that come your way to keep earning points! If you crash into an obstacle car, the left side of the screen will flash red, indicating a collision. For every obstacle car you hit, you'll lose 5 points.
Rule 3: Stay on the Road
Keep your car on the road and avoid veering into the grass! If you go out of bounds, the left side of the screen will flash yellow to alert you. For every moment spent out of bounds, you'll lose 2 points.
Rule 4: Watch Out for the Cops
When a cop icon appears at the top of your rear-view mirror, it means a cop is nearby. Keep your speed below 90! If your speed exceeds 90 while the cop is around, you'll lose 2 points.
